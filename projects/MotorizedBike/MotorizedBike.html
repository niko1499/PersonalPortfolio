Motorized Bike

Date: 2015
Motorized bike built with kit 2-stroke motor and 1940's Silver King Monark Bike frame. Three speed with internal back hub shifter. Home spoked bike wheels. Custom light circuitry to have functional front and rear blinking turning signals (555 timer), brake light, High beams, and horn. Custom machined and 3-D printed adapter brackets form mounting hardware. Top speed ~30mph.
