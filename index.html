About me

I am currently employeed as a full time Controlls Engineer at Chicago Dryer.

At WPI the Robotic Engineering program is a combination of Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Computer Science.

I became passionate about robotics in high school when I was a leading member of my school's FIRST robotics team West Side Boiler Invasion (FRC 461). During that time I became proficient in Computer Aided Design (CAD) and some manufacturing processes. I was also my team's driver for three years.

During my time at WPI, I discovered I enjoyed programming for robotics and began to switch my focus from Mechanical Engineering to Computer Science.
